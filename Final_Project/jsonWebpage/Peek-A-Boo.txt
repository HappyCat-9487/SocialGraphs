It is the first track in the group\'s second full-length album, \'\'[[Perfect Velvet]]\'\' and the third track in the group\'s second full-length album repackage, \'\'[[The Perfect Red Velvet]]\'\'.\n\n==Background==\nAfter finishing initial promotion for \'\'[[The Red Summer]]\'\' and wrapping up their first concert Red Room in August 2017, various South Korean news sites reported that Red Velvet is planning for their third comeback within the year on October 30. The announcement was then confirmed later that day by their home label S.M. Entertainment.\n\nS.M. Entertainment revealed the title of the group\'s new single through a teaser along with the title of their second full album, \'\'Perfect Velvet\'\' at midnight on November 8. Referencing the "velvet" half of the group\'s dual concept that affects the group\'s music and their image where their "red" side is brighter and livelier, producing upbeat songs such as their last single "[[Red Flavor]]" while their softer and more sophisticated "velvet" image spawned tracks predominantly of the R&B and ballad genre, S.M. Entertainment stated in an interview with X Sports News on November 8 that they intended to show "an upgraded version" of the group\'s "velvet" concept. The single is described as one of the group\'s "velvet" tracks but with hints of their "red" side which their company felt is the perfect formula for the concept.\n\n==Promotion==\nRed Velvet held a comeback showcase on the day of the song\'s release and promoted the song on several music programs, including \'\'Music Bank\'\', \'\'Inkigayo\'\', \'\'The Show\'\' and \'\'Show Champion\'\'. The group also performed the song on several year-end music awards, along with their single "Red Flavor".\n\n==Commercial performance==\nUpon its release, the song and the album received generally positive reviews from critics, who praised the group for their transition from their brighter and bubblier image from their last two singles "[[Rookie]]" and "Red Flavor" (both released in the same year), to a more mature sound.
