"Wannabe" employs a combination of house and hip-hop/pop production that is mixed with bubblegum-pop melodies, with the lyrics conveying themes stressing the importance of personal identity and individuality. The song was written and produced by Galactika, who also produced their debut track "DALLA DALLA." It runs for three minutes and twelve seconds.\n\n== Music video ==\nThe music video was released on March 9, 2020 along with the album. The music video was directed by the Naive Creative Production.
